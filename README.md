# 🎓 ClassPulse — Academic Intelligence

> A smart, student-friendly Progressive Web App (PWA) to track attendance and predict CIA scores — built to help college students stay ahead academically.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 📊 Attendance Tracker
- Track attendance subject-wise for each month
- Instantly see your **overall attendance percentage**
- Get alerts for subjects **below 75%** attendance
- View total number of subjects at a glance
- Select month to organize records by time period

### 🧮 CIA Prediction Tracker
- Enter your **CIA-1 marks** (out of 25)
- Automatically calculates the **required CIA-2 score** to maintain your target average
- Shows **predicted average** across both CIA exams per subject

### 📁 Data Management
- **Export to CSV** — download your attendance data as a spreadsheet
- **Print / Save as PDF** — keep a hard copy of your records
- **Clear by month** or wipe all data at once

### 📱 Progressive Web App (PWA)
- Works **offline** thanks to Service Worker caching
- Can be **installed on your phone** like a native app
- Fully **mobile responsive** design

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Option 1 — Open directly in browser
1. Clone this repository:
   ```bash

   ```
2. Open `index.html` in your browser — that's it!

1. Open the app in Chrome or Edge
2. Click the **install icon** in the address bar
3. Launch it like a native app from your home screen!

## 📖 How to Use

**Attendance Tracker:**
1. Select a month from the dropdown
2. Enter your subject name, total classes, and classes attended
3. Click **Calculate All** to see your attendance percentages
4. Add more subjects with the **+ Add Subject** button
5. Export your data as CSV or print it anytime

**CIA Prediction Tracker:**
1. Enter your subject name and CIA-1 marks (out of 25)
2. Click **Predict CIA-2** to see what score you need in your next exam
3. The predicted average helps you plan your study strategy
